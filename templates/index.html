About

We are interested in developing tools that can help us appreciate the beauty of intelligent biological systems. “beat” is the intersection of our two areas of research – brain waves and video games. We are at the Claremont Colleges, a consortium of undergraduate liberal arts colleges, where we seek to bridge ideas from neuroscience with those from other disciplines, such as psychology, philosophy, dance, and media studies. We believe that such dialogue can help improve our quality of life both individually and collectively.


Brain Waves

Brain Waves

We analyze brain waves recorded by hundreds of electrodes. While these waves were thought to be a blurry reflection of the underlying neuronal population, we find conditions in which these waves carry surprisingly precise information about ongoing behavior.


Video Games

Video Games

We developed a game to study how people discover solutions to hard problems. We find that humans display leaps of insight that distinguish them from current AI.
